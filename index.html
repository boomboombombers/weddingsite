How we met

We met in September, 2009 at the historic (well, locally historic) Deer Park Tarvern in Newark, Delaware while celebrating a friend's birthday. We immediately hit it off over a deep discussion of the joys of Mario Kart, photography, and motorcycles. We soon reconnected and started our journey through life together. As partners we have shared the joy of 3 homes, becoming an Aunt and Uncle to an awesome nephew and 3 dear neices, caring for 4 loving pets, and over a decade of adventures together. Our love has been a blessing and we look forward to a lifetime more.
